Ripple and Wormhole are expanding RLUSD to Layer 2 networks using the NTT standard, with testing beginning on Optimism, Base, Ink, and Unichain. RLUSD holds a New York Department of Financial Services Trust Company Charter and has a pending OCC charter application.
