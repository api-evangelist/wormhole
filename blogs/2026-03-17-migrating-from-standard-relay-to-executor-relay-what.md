Standard Relaying is deprecated, with automatic delivery shutting down on April 1, 2026, and Wormhole urges developers to migrate to the Executor relaying system. Executor offers broader chain support beyond EVM, lower on-chain costs through a stateless design, and a quote-based pricing model.
